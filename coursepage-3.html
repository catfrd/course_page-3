FREE CA DMV Diagnostic Test 2022

4.12 out of 5 • 2395 votes.
Based on 2022 CA driver's license manual Available in English, Spanish and Russian Instant feedback

The Pacific Coast Highway is calling your name, but if you don’t pass your driver’s exam, you’ll be stuck in the road like a Los Angeleno at rush hour. Our free diagnostic exam takes only the toughest questions that most Californians get wrong and speeds up your study time. Leaving the obvious ones out, you’ll be able to prepare more for the tricky questions that cause your frustration. Once you are at the California DMV, if you miss more than 8 out of the 46 test questions, you’ll be out of luck. So make sure you take less time to learn more.

Perfect for:

  • California Learner’s Permit
  • California Driver’s License
  • CA Senior Citizens’ Refresher Test
  • CA Driver’s License Renewal.

What to expect on the actual CA DMV exam:

  • 46 questions
  • 38 correct answers to pass
  • 83% passing score
  • Minimum age to apply: 15 ½

How does it work?

This practice test is a part of our comprehensive program, our best attempt at replicating the actual DMV permit/driver's license test you will be taking. The best part? A lot of the tests are free - just like this one! We recommend using them to get a feel for the format and the style of the DMV tests, and then checking out our Premium program if you want to take your studying to the next level (and dramatically increase your chances of passing the real test the first time around.)
